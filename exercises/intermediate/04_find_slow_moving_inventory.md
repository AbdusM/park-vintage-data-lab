# Mission 5: Find slow-moving inventory

## Goal

Use simple data filters to find older in-stock items.

## Tasks

1. Open `data/raw/inventory.csv`.
2. Compare `status` and `date_added`.
3. Identify items with status `In Stock` and an early date.
4. Decide two markdown recommendations for movement.

## Acceptance criteria

- You can point to at least two slow-moving items.
- You can write a recommendation that helps owners make a decision.
